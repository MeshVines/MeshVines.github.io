What is Meshvines?

A REST API for data-driven winery operation.

Wine Fermentation Kinetic Model

The Boulton model (1979) describes the kinetics of juice to wine based on commmonly-used wine yeast strains of Saccharomyces Cerevisiae and has been used on hundreds of fermentations over many years at the University of California, Davis Teaching and Research Winery.

Parameter Estimation

The parameters of the Boulton model are solved for in the time and resource constraints of a REST API.

Current and Future Heat and Carbon Dioxide Release

The heat and carbon dioxide release rates and evolution curves are estimated from the fermentation rate.

Current and Future Ethanol Emissions

The ethanol emissions are estimated.

Total Winery Loads

The fermentation loads (heat, co2 and ethanol emissions) can be summed accross multiple fermentations.

Unit and Density Conversions

Multiple units are supported. Functions are supported to convert between density scales. The denisty can also be calculated from differential pressure for tanks with two pressure transducers.
